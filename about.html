Hey — I'm Synaptical. I'm an independent musician, content creator, creative writer, gamer, and more. If you don't like some of my creations, I'm sure I have something for you... somewhere... who knows?

The music I create isn't bound by a singlular label or category, and I prefer to keep it that way. I love indulging in many different genres and attempting to put my own creativity into it. I love music.

As for the rest of my creative work, I think exploring for yourself would be the best way to discover who I am. Perhaps I may inspire you to go beyond what I have done, hell, I hope you do.
